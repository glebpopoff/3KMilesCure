BIKE SEBRING 12-HOUR RACE / Maria

Feb 14-15, 2015

The gun goes off at 6:30 a.m. EST on February 14th for Maria’s 12- hour race at the Sebring International Raceway. Clip in and pedal with her as she races through the next 12 hours. Maria’s race allows drafting, so expect to see her wheel-to-wheel with the leaders of the pack and experience the race through the Google Glass she wears. Drop messages of encouragement and donations toward her fundraising goal on her race map - she’ll get them when she passes the location you choose.
