HOPE LOVES ACTION.

Especially on Valentine’s day. Take action with Maria and Rob as they pedal through grueling 12 and 24 hour races in Sebring, Florida to raise money to end brain cancer. Share encouragement and donate in real time along each route.

BIKE SEBRING 12-HOUR RACE // Maria

The gun goes off at 6:30 a.m. EST on February 14th for Maria’s 12-hour race at the Sebring International Raceway. Clip in and pedal with her as she races through the next 12 hours. Maria’s race allows drafting, so expect to see her wheel-to-wheel with the leaders of the pack and experience the race through the Google Glass she wears. Drop messages of encouragement and donations toward her fundraising goal on her race map - she’ll get them when she passes the location you choose.

BIKE SEBRING 24-HOUR RACE // Rob

Welcome Rob White to the 3000 Miles to a Cure team! He is taking on the 24-hour race at Sebring to kick-off his solo Race Across America effort for the cause. Not onl is Rob an accomplished ultra marathon cyclist with an all-star team of crew and supporters, he is passionate about ending brain cancer. A close friend’s son has battled the disease and Rob is using is cycling to fight for a cure. Drop messages of encouragement and donations toward Rob’s fundraising goal on his race map - he’ll get them when he passes the location you choose.

RACE WITH ROB

We’re excited to invite you into Rob’s race in a new way through Google Glass and this platform. Thank you for joining us. The encouragement you share through messages and donations will inspire and motivate Rob during the most challenging moments of the event. Rob and our team take on extremely challenging ultra marathon events like the Sebring 24-hour race because they are a powerful metaphor for the battles being waged against brain cancer by those who fight it and those racing to cure it. Together, will be a force that changes the future for those diagnosed with brain cancer. Thank you for coming alongside us, persevering with us, sharing hope and taking action to create change.
